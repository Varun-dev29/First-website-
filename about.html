MY AND MY PARTNER STORY

At just 18 years old, Varun and his friend Saket embarked on a journey fueled by passion and creativity. Fresh out of school, both shared a love for technology and a desire to do something on their own.
With a clear vision, they co-founded WebElites, a freelancing venture that would allow them to put their web design and development skills to good use.
Starting from scratch, they worked tirelessly to build their portfolio, taking on small projects that gradually grew in complexity. Their passion for crafting WordPress websites led them to gain a reputation for delivering sleek, user-friendly websites, no matter the challenge. Every project became a learning experience, and every satisfied client a testament to their dedication.
Despite their young age, Varun and Saket quickly realized the potential of freelancing. They weren’t just building websites—they were helping small businesses and entrepreneurs bring their visions to life online. As WebElites grew, so did their ambition, setting them on a path to build their brand, enhance their skills, and explore bigger opportunities.
Through persistence, learning, and collaboration, they transformed WebElites from a small idea into a growing, recognized name in the world of freelance web development.
